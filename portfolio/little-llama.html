The Little Llama

The Little Llama is a game created for the Ludum Dare 38 game jam by a team of 3 within 48 hours. You play as a llama on a journey to find itself by solving puzzles using its telekinetic abilities that are unexplained and unacknowledged.

Being a game jam project, this was a fairly small endavour, with the largest challenge lying in getting things functional and usable.

Ludum Dare 2017

Role: Game Designer, Artist, Sound Designer
