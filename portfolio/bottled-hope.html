Solar Brightbox

The Solar Brightbox! A product we've come to enhance and develop through multiple commissions. Making it lighter, better and simpler over the 2 years since we first produced it together with Pernod Ricard Malaysia. Through the Bottled Hope campaign, we distributed the lamps to villages outside of the electricity grid, or were disaster stricken. Which at that time, were the East Coast Malaysia floods and Nepali earthquake.

Through another commission by the French Embassy here, Biji-biji's Electronics team were able to enhance the system. Keeping it's upcycled element of reusing glass bottles, but making it much lighter and simpler.

By making the Brightbox system simple, we hope that the receiving villagers could easily copy and reproduce the lamp. Staying true to our Open Source commitments, we made the schematics available online, where anyone has the rights to study, change and distribute this design.
