Orkestra Melayang

Directly translating to "Drifting Orchestra" from Malay, Orkestra Melayang was inspired by a flyer for an orchestral recital I came across in Vienna.

The elegant curves, aggressive angles, and sharp points in Orkestra are primarily meant as a series of features to draw attention and keep it. Swooping ligatures were added later to soften the silhouettes of certain letter pairings.

With Melayang, the plan was to do the opposite of what I had done with Orkestra; which is to say creating someting gentle, flowing, and dainty, almost.

All in all, the pairing of the two fonts comes together in near perfect harmony, the characteristics of each balancing themselves out.
