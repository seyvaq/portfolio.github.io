Light a Way

Light a Way is an idle mobile clicker game where you play the role of a little girl who runs into a fairy who imbues her with powers by giving her a staff and instructing her to use her powers to dispatch shadow creatures that threaten the world.

Working with the multidisciplinary team, my work was to ideate and flesh out design concepts in order to construct the experience we were all aiming for. We achieved this with over 2 million+ downloads across Android and IOS.
