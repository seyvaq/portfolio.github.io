Tepi Jalan

Tepi Jalan (directly translating from Malay to "beside the road") was initially a joke that started with an innocent "Hey, how wild would it be if we started and ran a bar?"

3 months later, after loads of planning, preparation, and execution of everything pre-production, Tepi Jalan looked much more fleshed out than anyone from the team had initially expected.

What started as a small pop-up bar ended up being a long term project built around designing, crafting, and shipping cocktails, mocktails, and other sorts of drinks across the entire Klang Valley.
