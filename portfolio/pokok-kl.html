Pokok.KL

Pokok is the flagship café of the Brick House Group. Patrons enjoy a mix of drool-worthy dishes with flavours from the east and west, freshly baked cakes and quality roasted coffee in a thoughtfully designed light-filled space.

As the head of design, a lot of my team's work was based around building a household brand around the Pokok name. From menu design, to table arrangements, to event layouts, to social media posts, a massive amount of research and work went into the crafting and refining of the Pokok.KL brand.

The pandemic presented the team with the biggest challenge we'd faced till date, so we built entirely new distribution channels in the form of a new website, online grocery store, online delivery platform, and a customer loyalty program.
