Vandure

The pandemic hit the wedding industry hard. We saw a gap waiting to be filled, and acted immediately to build a solution to a problem many were only just realizing they had. Cloud Weddings was a venture into the virtualization of weddings, a very traditional industry.

Through the course of development, we landed on a platform-agnostic, multi-faceted, fully interactive virtual platform for couples to share their most important moments with those that mattered to them the most.

From fully virtual weddings, to hybrid weddings with guests both physically present and attending virtually, Cloud Weddings quickly found a niche with those that had friends or family overseas that could not make it to their wedding, running over a dozen cloud weddings within the first months of its inception.
