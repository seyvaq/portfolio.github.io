Vandure

What started off as an offshoot of another product blossomed into its own thing with Vandure. Built off the Cloud Wedding codebase, Vandure was established as a fully integrated solution for teachers, instructors, and coaches looking to run classes online.

Through many iterations, Vandure ended up becoming a multi-faceted product, going into the link-in-bio space as well as venturing into becoming a fully featured micro-site provider. With the intention of building a one-stop solution to multiple problems our customer base was facing, Vandure was truly a an endavour of note.
