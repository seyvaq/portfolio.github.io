Web Lab

Web Lab brings the magic of the web to life through 5 Chrome Experiments. Each experiment was not only open to the world online, but also live in the Science Museum, London.

Our role was creating the brand identity used across the project, as well as designing and producing the web-based experience that connects the exhibition to the online world.

The experiments ran for a year during which time they were visited by everyone from will.i.am to the children of Anson Primary School, who were even inspired to create their own Web Lab!

Google Creative Lab 2012
